## Honest caveats (so no one is misled)

These are limitations of the *law and the technology*, not carve-outs Anthropic
takes for itself:

- **Copyrightability.** In some jurisdictions, material generated with
  substantial machine assistance and without sufficient human authorship may not
  be protectable by copyright at all. Where a work (or part of it) is not
  copyrightable, "ownership" is moot for that part regardless of any assignment.
  The human design decisions, selection, arrangement, editing, and original
  writing throughout this project are the human-authored contribution.
- **Non-exclusivity of similar outputs.** Anthropic's terms note that outputs are
  not unique: other users may receive the same or similar outputs from the model.
  An assignment of Anthropic's interest in *your* outputs does not stop the model
  from producing similar text for someone else.
- **You are responsible for outputs.** The customer, not Anthropic, is
  responsible for the outputs and for ensuring they do not infringe third-party
  rights. This project deliberately references **no external intellectual
  property**; all names, prose, art, and audio are its own.
- **Terms change.** The linked pages are the living source of truth; this summary
  reflects them as understood at the time of writing and may lag later revisions.
